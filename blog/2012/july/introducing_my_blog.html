Introducing my Blog15th July, 2012

Just thought I'd create a quick post to introduce my blog to the world properly. Now I have a place where I can post self-absorbed ramblings on the internet in a way that suits me best! Hooray!

I created a pretty terrible system for creating and publishing posts for this blog. Creating a new post is as simple as typing ./post "Title of Post" in my terminal. Then, I can edit the post before using ./publish to publish the blog post to the github-hosted blog, and annoy everyone on twitter about it.

I have tried to keep everything as simple as possible. Hence the simple design and total lack of comments. If you want to throw insults at me in response to one of my blog posts, I guess you could just shout at me on twitter or something.

This blog is supposed to be a place for me to throw my thoughts, which are a scarce resource these days. I may write a bit more about how this blog works, or doesn't work, in a later blog post. For now, though, I don't really care much.

The links at the bottom of blog posts serve as sufficient navigation for this harberdash scratching exhibition. Or at least I think so anyways.

That's all for now! I'll probably forget about this thing soon enough.
